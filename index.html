Creamy White Sauce Pasta

This delicious pasta is made with fresh vegetables and a rich creamy sauce. Perfect for dinner!
Ingredients include pasta, milk, butter, flour, garlic, capsicum, and cheese.

Procedure:

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until soft.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and add flour to prepare white sauce.
  3. Slowly add milk while stirring to avoid lumps.
  4. Add chopped vegetables and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Mix boiled pasta and add grated cheese.

Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful rice dish cooked with aromatic spices. Best served hot with raita.
Ingredients include basmati rice, chicken, onions, yogurt, and biryani masala.

Procedure:

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices.
  2. Cook rice separately until 80% done.
  3. Layer rice and chicken in a pot.
  4. Cook on low flame for 20 minutes (dum cooking).
  5. Serve hot.

Fluffy Pancakes

Soft and fluffy pancakes perfect for breakfast. Quick and easy to prepare!
Ingredients include flour, milk, eggs, sugar, and baking powder.

Procedure:

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. Add milk and eggs to make smooth batter.
  3. Heat a pan and pour a ladle of batter.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  5. Serve with honey or maple syrup.

Healthy Veg Salad

A refreshing and nutritious salad for a light meal. Ideal for weight management.
Ingredients include cucumber, tomato, carrot, lettuce, and lemon juice.

Procedure:

  1. Chop all vegetables evenly.
  2. Mix them in a large bowl.
  3. Add salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
  4. Toss well before serving.

Chocolate Brownie

A rich and fudgy chocolate dessert loved by everyone. Best enjoyed warm with ice cream.
Ingredients include cocoa powder, flour, sugar, butter, and eggs.

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix melted butter and sugar.
  3. Add eggs and cocoa powder.
  4. Fold in flour gently.
  5. Bake for 25–30 minutes.
